Bio

Jade Thornton is a 20 year old busker / singer-songwriter based in South London. She has been singing since she can remember & started to learn guitar at the age of 14, after convincing her parents to get lessons. Song-writing was an obstacle for her to overcome, but went on to place top 10 in the country in the ‘Song Academy’s Young Songwriter’ competition with her song ‘Only Me’ in 2017 & took part in the 2017 Mayor of London’s ‘Busk in London’ competition. After gaining qualifications in music performance and music technology, at college and trying various jobs, she decided to take the plunge and do music full time. Now Jade regularly plays private functions, weddings and busks in her local high streets & London’s busiest overground stations.
