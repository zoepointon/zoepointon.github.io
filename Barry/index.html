Why You’re Here

I know right, you’re busy during the week with life, this is why I created ‘The Weekend Career Coach’ (Career, Executive or Life Coaching) so that you have access to the same quality professional coaching services you might only normally get during office hours.

Your Time Matters

Let’s face it, you spend so much time at work it’s hugely important that you enjoy it or at the very least get something from it, perhaps that enriches your life and fulfils your sense of purpose. As your coach I can help you decide what is important to you and help you chart a course to achieving this.

How We Would Work

Together we will figure out what’s important to you and needs to change, agree specific goals, talk through challenges and barriers you are facing and come up with steps to success together.

A Bit About Me

Not only am I a chartered professional with over 20 years experience helping people develop themselves and their careers, I am a certified Executive Coach with years of experience supporting not just big corporate challenges but also with the important day to day life stuff. I am also a qualified Mental Heath First Aider so can help support you and your teams with Health and Wellbeing initiatives. A ‘corporate robot’ I am not, but I have continued to have a successful career, working at executive leadership level for some of the biggest brand names out there.


In my spare time I am usually with freinds and family, visiting new places, learning about something new or just simply relaxing recharging my own batteries.

My Style

All my coaching is done face to face, usually over a cuppa, in a very real, authentic human way.

My Own Continuous Learning

I too have a coach, this helps keep me focused and anchored. I love to learn, I am always on the look out for anything to do with people and personal development. Over my career I have attended many courses, workshops and CPD activities.
