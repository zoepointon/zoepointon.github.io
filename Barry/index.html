A Bit About Me

I love to people watch (not in a creepy way), I just love watching how people interact and how relationships play out, I get a lot from meeting new people and find out about their unique stories, these can be so enriching to the soul. I also love to travel seeing new places and meet the inhabitants, it can be a real life changing experience. Friends and family are important to me.


Work wise, Not only am I a chartered professional with over 20 years experience helping people develop themselves and their careers, I am a certified Executive coach with years of experience supporting with not just big corporate challenges but in the important day to day life stuff too. A ‘corporate robot’ I am not, but I continue to have a successful career, having worked at executive leadership level with some of the biggest brand names out there.
