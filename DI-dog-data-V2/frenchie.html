Three Recognised Colours

The French Bulldog club in the UK only accepts three colours; Pied, Fawn and Brindle.

Pied

Fawn

Brindle

Colours Not to Buy

Any other colours indicate bad breeding practices. Any dogs the following colours are likely to have genetic deformities that affect the health and temperament of the dog; Blue, Chocolate, Blue Pied, Blue Fawn, Blue Brindle, Black & Tan. and Mearle.

Colours Advertised

Most of the colours advertised are unrecognised. This indicates that there is lots of detrimental breeding occurring.
