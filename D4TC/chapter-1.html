Chapter 1:
2050

In 2020 the United Nations formed a new intergovernmental organisation called IGo. Their primary objective is to govern and maintain the internet. In 2025 IGo made 6G free and accessible for everyone across the globe.

Due to the rise in sea levels London has become submerged.

Waves of innovation

This triggers the UK government to invested heavily into sustainability, in particular Marine futures.

In 2040 London became the first floating city in the world.

In 2050 Bio Tec hits the mainstream. The way humans interact with the internet has moved beyond screens.
