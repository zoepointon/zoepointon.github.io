What is BAOS?

Brachycephalic Airway Obstruction Syndrome (BAOS) is a genetic disorder that effects flat nosed dog breeds. It makes breathing hard for dogs such as; Pugs and French Bulldogs. This disorder is is the result of selective breeding overtime.

How Many Dogs does it Impact?

50% of dogs bred to have flat noses suffer from BASO.

Side Effects

Loud Breathing and Snoring

Exercise and Heat Exertion

Gagging and Vomiting

Flat Nosed Dog, Market Share
